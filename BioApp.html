  1. Course Pre-Requisites: Junior or Senior Status. BIOL 3400; CHEM 1211; 1212; PHYS I & II sequence

  2. If selected for the Service Learning Internship Course, you will need to go through a backgroundcheck. The background check form can be found on the Service Learning Internship site on My.GGC. You will need to complete this AFTER you are selected for the course. If accepted to the program, you will need to sign the form under "applicant's signature", take the completed form to Student Accounts and pay $25 for the background check. Staple the receipt and background check together and return to Dr. Allison D'Costa (adcosta@ggc.edu) or Dr. Clay Runck. Final acceptance is contingent on "passing" a background check.

  3. If selected for the Service Learning Internship Course, you will need to get insurance for $7.00 from PAGE Professional Association of Georgia Educators

  4. Service Learning Internships require that you spend half your time at a participating elementary school implementing science labs, and the rest of your time at GGC preparing the lesson plan, labs and course delivery. The internship course meets on Tuesdays and Thursdays. On the days you are in the elementary school, your hours will be 12:30-3:00 pm, and, at GGC, the hours will be 12:30-1:45pm. The hours in the school are longer as you need to set-up equipment for the labs and clean up after lab. You will also spend several hours at home preparing materials for the labs. (NOTE: The course hours are different from what is listed in Banner).

  5. Service Learning Interns are expected to act and dress professionally during all visits to the elementary schools, and arrive on time at the elementary school (which is about 2.5 mi from GGC).

  6. An important criteria for selection is public speaking ability. If you are not comfortable speaking in front of people, this internship is not for you. In a short interview, you must describe how this experience will benefit your career.
